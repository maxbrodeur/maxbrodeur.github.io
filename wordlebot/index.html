Description

Following the trend, my parents started playing the Wordle game. If you're not familiar, it's like the Mastermind boardgame but with words: there's a secret word you have to guess in at most 6 attempts. The letter is colored green if its in the right position, yellow if it's not at the correct position but in the word, and black if it isn't in the word. However, a difference is the guess must be a valid word, thus can't be some jibber jabber such as "jmkals".

Instead of attempting to beat my Dad's glorious 3 attempt guesses, and instead of studying for my midterm, I spent the weekend coding a Wordle Bot while learning about Python's webscraping capabilities with Selenium.

In hindsight, this probably wasn't the most efficient strategy... My midterm still went pretty well though! :)
